The video features an orangutan named Sandra engaging in a handwashing ritual that mimics the behavior of human zookeepers. Seated on the grass beside a large green ball in an outdoor enclosure, Sandra sits in a blue plastic tub filled with water and soap, using the basin to clean her hands. She holds a bottle of soap in one hand and occasionally uses a sponge or white cloth in the other while scrubbing her hands. This mimicking behavior is reportedly inspired by observations made during the COVID-19 pandemic. Sandra is consistently seen washing her hands while sitting on the ground in the enclosure.
